Visit Campus

CGU’s wide-ranging, customizable programs and renowned faculty may be our main selling points, but our scenic tree-lined campus and Southern California allure aren’t so bad either. We’re a great fit for ambitious learners who also demand an active social life, gorgeous natural surroundings, and vibrant culture.

We look forward to meeting you in person. You may attend one of several scheduled Information Sessions throughout the year (scroll down to view a listing of Admission Events), take advantage of our Class Preview option and sit in on one of our courses, or meet us online at a Virtual Information Session. Contact our Office of Admissions to schedule a visit to discover all that CGU has to offer.

Getting Here

From the 10 Freeway

  • Exit Indian Hill Blvd. and head north
  • Turn right on W. Arrow Hwy. (0.2 miles)
  • Turn left on College Ave. (0.9 miles)
  • Turn right on E. 10th St.

From the 210 Freeway East

  • Exit Towne Ave.
  • Turn right on Towne Ave. (0.9 miles)
  • Turn left on Foothill Blvd. (1.4 miles)
  • Turn right on Dartmouth Ave.
  • Turn right on E. 10th St.

From the 210 Freeway West

  • Exit Baseline Rd.
  • Turn right on Baseline Rd. (0.7 miles)
  • Turn left on N. Mills Ave (1.0 miles)
  • Turn right on Foothill Blvd (0.3 miles)
  • Turn left on Dartmouth Ave.
  • Turn right on E. 10th St.
Airports

The most convenient airport to Claremont is Ontario International Airport.

Transportation from the airport to area hotels can be arranged through the telephones at the ground transportation board in the baggage claim areas. Taxi and shuttle service can also be found at these terminals. Reservations are not required for door-to-door shuttle service, but if you may call ahead:

  • Econoride: 877-413-2666
  • Inland Express: 909-626-6599
  • Prime Time: 800-733-8267
  • Southern California Coach: 800-232-6224
  • Supershuttle: 800-933-6901

Other airports in the area are the Bob Hope (Burbank) Airport, Los Angeles International (LAX), and the John Wayne (Orange County) Airport, each approximately an hour’s drive from Claremont.

Parking Information

Campus Lots
Visitor parking is available in the parking lot on the northwest corner of Dartmouth and 11th avenues and in the lot on the northeast corner of College Avenue and Eighth Street.

Street Parking
Street parking on CGU’s campus does not require a parking permit. However, street-parked vehicles cannot be left overnight. Single-night overnight passes may be obtained from the City of Claremont. Contact the city at 909-399-5411.

Neighborhood Agreement
The Claremont Colleges have established certain agreements with the local neighborhood regarding parking practices and privileges. Students and visitors may not park vehicles on the west side of College Avenue or within the neighborhoods west of College Avenue.
