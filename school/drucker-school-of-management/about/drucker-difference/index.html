Influential careers start at Drucker. We can’t wait to see how you change the world.

Peter Drucker taught us to see an entire organization from the customer’s point of view. In our case, that means our focus is on you—the student—and your ability to graduate and lead others as effectively as possible. We watch the market closely to ensure our programs are grounded in what’s relevant today, and we adapt quickly so that you remain competitive, highly employable, and socially conscious.

Our graduates enter the workplace with a core set of multidisciplinary management skills, including strategic decision-making, organizational development, market and data analysis, and self-management, among countless others. With these skills, you’ll be able to hit the ground running and accelerate along your career path the moment you finish your program.

SKILLS FOR THE REAL WORLD

Drucker students have spread far and wide in pursuit of their managerial passions, from interning in prominent organizations throughout the United States to conducting field studies internationally. On campus, we provide hands-on training through our centers, labs, and institutes, such as:

  • The Game Lab
  • The Customer Lab
  • The Global Family Business Institute

Here you can develop prototypes or new ideas and commercialize them yourself or embed them into an organization. By setting assignments that apply concepts to real organizations and allowing you to tailor your coursework to suit your own professional and personal goals, we ensure that you receive an education full of practical, real-world relevance.

A PERSONALIZED EXPERIENCE

We are committed to your post-graduate success, and we align your course of study with your unique career goals. The Drucker School is based at Claremont Graduate University, which allows you to enhance your education with a diverse array of additional courses and programs. The university supports a transdisciplinary approach to learning as well as dual degrees.

We also have degree partnerships with several prominent institutions, such as:

  • Sotheby’s Institute of Art
  • Art Center College of Design
  • Southwestern Law School

Whatever your real-world ambitions, we’ll be right by your side to make them a reality.

Faculty and Classes

Our faculty is comprised of industry leaders and globally recognized scholars who produce high-impact research across a range of topics: strategy, revitalization, leadership, finance, innovation, marketing, mindfulness, and many other areas of management.

Classes are small and interactive, with a focus on fostering discussion and collaboration. At the Drucker School, you will build collegiate relationships with faculty and classmates that last well beyond graduation and throughout your professional career.

CAREER SUPPORT

We are deeply invested in the long-term success of every single one of our students. We want nothing more than to watch you put the Drucker management principles into practice as you leverage your career to create positive change in the world.

As a student, you will work with our Career Development Office from day one to put you on a career path aligned with your interests and ambitions. You will find enthusiastic mentors among our faculty and alumni, both before and after graduation. And wherever you go in the world, you will never be far from like minded executives who share the Drucker philosophy and values that have informed your management style.
